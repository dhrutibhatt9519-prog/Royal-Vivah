NGO

Usually happiness lies in simple things! Laddoo Foundation is about spreading sweetness around.

“The best way to find yourself is to lose yourself in the service of others." : Mahatma Gandhi

We are a group of people who donate Laddoo in slum areas every Sunday in the quest of bringing smile to those needful faces.

There are many organizations in the world working on food & hunger. We are not associated with any of those organizations; neither do we claim to be working for those causes.

With a single aim to bring positive change in the society we live, Laddoo Foundation took its shape.

We might not change the entire course of life of the needy, but we hope, that a little smile would bring lot of joy in their hearts, thus in this society. If those poor people are able to feel happiness for a day or even for a moment, our purpose is fulfilled.

Moreover, Laddoo Foundation not only supplies food to street people but we also work towards the betterment on other subjects like education, environment and art.

Our ultimate aim remains to bring out the holistic development of an individual, concentrating on poor and needy.
