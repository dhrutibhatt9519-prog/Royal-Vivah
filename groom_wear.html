Groom Wear

When it’s come to a wedding, people think that it’s all about bride. Today’s Indian groom knows to share the spotlight with his bride with prominent style sense. There has been way too much said already about coordinating outfit colors between the bride and the groom. While with most people the general trend is to match outfit colors together,you may want to put a bit of thought!!

An Indian groom’s outfit is dressier and he has more options than his western attire, which mostly include the standard tuxedo. You can choose from options like traditional sherwani, dhoti kurta, kurta pyjama, jodhpuri, indo-western suits or three piece suits.

Groom wear – THE DAPPER GROOM. Many Indian grooms are matching their outfit with their beautiful bride to look like a one soul person. Grooms are nowadays accessorizing their outfit by brooches and tiered necklace or pocket napkin. And yes if you think anarkali is made for women then you are mistaken you need to see dashing RANVEER SINGH in “BAJIRAO MASTANI”. Grooms are opting for some royal look even. Designer jodhpuri and safas are lovely surprises in men’s ensembles these days.

Even the velvet fabric which gives you the velvet touch offers the desired look.
